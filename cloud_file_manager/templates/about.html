Photo & Video Management Application

Making memories accessible and organized

Our Mission

People often face the challenge of finding and organizing their photos and videos. With multiple devices and cloud storage services, it can become overwhelming to manage all of our memories in one place.

Our aim is to develop a solution that addresses the common problem of finding and organizing photos and videos by simplifying the process of uploading, organizing, and storing photos and videos, making it easier for users to access and enjoy their memories.

Benefits

  • Easy upload, management, and organization of photos and videos in one place
  • User-friendly interface
  • Access to memories from anywhere with an internet connection
  • Secure storage on Google Cloud Platform
  • No need to manage multiple devices or cloud storage services

FAQ

  • Q: What file formats are accepted?
    A: We accept jpeg, png, gif, mp4, and webm files.
